# Meeting Notes — Product Strategy

**Date**: June 25, 2026
**Attendees**: Sarah, David, Maria, James

## Key Decisions

1. Prioritize mobile app for Q3 launch over desktop redesign
2. Defer internationalization to Q4 — not enough demand signal yet
3. Approve $40K budget for user research study in July

## Discussion Summary

### Mobile App Priority

Sarah presented the customer feedback data showing 67% of support tickets come from users attempting to use the web app on mobile devices. The team agreed that a native mobile experience would have the highest impact on retention.

Maria flagged that the current API isn't optimized for mobile — payload sizes are too large and there's no pagination on the timeline endpoint. She estimates 2 weeks of API work before the mobile team can start.

### Internationalization

David shared the revenue breakdown by region:

| Region | Revenue | % of Total |
|---|---|---|
| North America | $38.2M | 81% |
| Europe | $6.1M | 13% |
| APAC | $2.9M | 6% |

The team agreed that with only 19% of revenue from non-English markets, i18n can wait until the EU expansion push in Q4.

### User Research

James proposed a 4-week qualitative study with 20 customers across three segments (SMB, mid-market, enterprise). The goal is to validate three hypotheses:

- Users want collaborative editing (not just sharing)
- The current onboarding flow loses people at step 3 (integrations)
- Enterprise buyers need better admin controls before purchasing

> The research findings will feed directly into the Q4 planning cycle. James will have preliminary results by August 15.

# Q2 2026 Business Review

## Executive Summary

Revenue grew 18% year-over-year to $47.2M, driven by strong enterprise adoption and the launch of our Professional tier. Net retention rate improved to 124%, reflecting successful upsell motion. Operating margin expanded to 22%, up from 17% in Q1.

Key highlights:

- **ARR**: $189M (up from $160M in Q1)
- **New enterprise logos**: 34 (target: 30)
- **Net retention**: 124% (target: 120%)
- **Employee count**: 412 (up from 385)

3. **SOC 2 Type II Certification** — Completed audit with zero exceptions. Published report available to customers.
